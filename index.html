About Us

Highlighting Youth in Journalism

The Youth Press Project connects Baltimore students with journalism workshops, press rights education, and resources to launch their own youth-led news organizations. We're empowering youth to cover their communities through their voices.

Free Journalism Workshops

Join our series of seven youth-led workshops covering reporting, writing, visual storytelling, podcasting, and how to launch your own student news organization.

Student Press Rights

Learn about Maryland's student press freedom laws and your constitutional protections. Understand your rights to report without censorship.

Mentorship & Resources

Connect with professional journalists, editors, and youth organizers who will guide you through building and sustaining your own media outlet.
